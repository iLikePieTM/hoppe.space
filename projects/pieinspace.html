High Hopes

Pie in space, the sucessful Kickstarter

The Pie in Space project involved sending a payload attached to a weather balloon over 100,000 ft. into the stratosphere and then recovering it. The payload consisted of a GoPro, GPS tracking device, and apple pie inside a capsule made from a cooler. The capsule was then connected to a parachute which was intern connected to the high-altitude weather balloon. After launch the balloon rose more than 100,000 ft. above eastern Washington before bursting in the low pressure. Then it was tracked via GPS on its decent before being recovered.

A video of the trip can be found here.

The Pie in Space, my first “big” project, grew from a passion for space. Taking countless hours of planning and raising money this project had a huge influence on the person I am today.
