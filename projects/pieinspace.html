High Hopes

Pie in space, the sucessful Kickstarter.

Pie in Space, my first “big” project, grew from a passion for aerospace. Taking countless hours of planning, designing, and raising money this project has had a huge impact on the person I am today.

For the project sent a payload consisting of a GoPro, GPS tracking device, and apple pie into the stratosphere. The payload was connected to a high-altitude weather balloon and parachute to slow its descent. After launch, the balloon rose more than 100,000 feet above Eastern Washington before bursting in the low air pressure. Using the GPS transmitter to track its movements I recovered the payload in a dusty field.
