Code Name:

"Fly Like an Eagle"

The official CubeSat club at Embry-Riddle Prescott.

EagleSat is a student lead CubeSat project supported by the NASA Space Grant Program and Embry-Riddle. Our current mission, EagleSat 2, is a 3U CubeSat set to launch in 2021 as part of NASA's CubeSat Launch Initiative. The spacecraft will carry dual scientific payloads; one designed to detect and track cosmic rays and the other to test how space radiation can affect computer memory.

As the Structures Team Lead is my job to ensure the spacecraft survives launch and orbit. To accomplish this task my team and I have designed and built a custom 3U CubeSat structure for the mission that is both lighter and more space efficient than any commercially available structure. We will take this structure and submit it to a bombardment of thermal, shock, and vibration tests to ensure it is space ready. In addition to the structure, we also manage the spacecraft's thermal control systems to keep all systems within optimal temperature ranges in the vacuum of space.

To get updates on the status of EagleSat head over to our Facebook page.
