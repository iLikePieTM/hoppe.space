Code Name:

The Spruce Duck

A simple quadcopter frame made from cheap laser cut wood parts

My wooden quadcopter (named after the Hughes H-4 Hercules) was a project I started while attending Highline College. It took a few prototypes and quite a few snapped arms but my final design has held up exceptionally well despite my poor flying skills. If you have any interest in experimenting with a simple quadcopter download the step file and laser cut your own!
