About Me

As the big bold letters above stated I’m Alex, an engineering student with a love for all things STEAM and a passion for aerospace. As a student at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University in Prescott, AZ I am be studying Mechanical Engineering with a minor in aerospace.

I have participated in numerus organizations and events; everything from the Washington Aerospace Scholars to FIRST Robotics. I addition I’ve worked on a variety projects including a custom, laser cut quadcopter and a capsule that sent an apple pie into the stratosphere. That’s just the start and I have many more planned, you can see my past projects and ideas for new ones below.

It is my dream to work in the aerospace industry after graduation. I am particularly interested in the research and development of Uninhabited Aerial Vehicles which I believe have the capability to revolutionize the worlds of transportation in the form of fast, efficient deliveries in remote areas and entertainment in the form of drone racing. Above all I wish to open source this amazing technology and make it more accessible and approachable for everyone.
