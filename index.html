About Me

I’m Alex, an engineering student with a love for all things STEM and a passion for crazy projects. At Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University in Prescott, AZ I am studying Mechanical Engineering with a robotics focus and a minor in Computer Science.

I have extensive experience in the STEM field having worked with Embry-Riddle's EagleSat program, FIRST Robotics, and the Washington Aerospace Scholars. Most recently, I worked at General Dynamics as a Mechanical Engineering Co-Op and developed electromechanical systems for challenging environments. I have further developed my skills through a variety of personal projects encompassing everything from a laser-cut quadcopter to sending an apple pie into the stratosphere. You can see my past projects and ideas for new ones below.

It is my dream to enter the engineering R&D industry after graduation and apply my skills to the field of robotics and other complex electromechanical systems. This area of engineering excites me as I enjoy the opportunity to apply all my mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering skills.
